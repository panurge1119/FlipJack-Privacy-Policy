# FlipJack Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 9, 2026

FlipJack ("we," "our," or "us") is a tvOS app for Apple TV that displays now-playing music information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## 1. Information We Collect
FlipJack does **not** collect, sell, or share personal data for advertising or cross-app tracking.

FlipJack stores the following data locally on your device to provide app functionality:
- App preferences (for example: tile size, text colors, background image, refresh interval, selected music source)
- Optional Last.fm username
- Optional Roon bridge URL and selected Roon zone
- Spotify refresh token (if you connect Spotify), stored securely in Apple Keychain

## 2. How We Use Information
We use this information only to:
- Configure app appearance and behavior
- Connect to the music service(s) you choose
- Display currently playing song, artist, and album information
- Support optional playback controls where supported by the connected service

## 3. Network Requests and Third Parties
To provide core functionality, FlipJack may communicate with:
- Apple Music (MusicKit)
- Spotify Web API (if connected)
- Last.fm API (if connected)
- Cloudflare Worker endpoints used for Spotify pairing
- Your optional local Roon bridge on your home network

FlipJack does not use third-party ad SDKs or analytics SDKs.

## 4. Data Sharing and Sale
- We do **not** sell personal information.
- We do **not** share personal information with advertisers or data brokers.
- Data is sent only as needed to services you intentionally connect and use.

## 5. Data Retention and Your Controls
- Local app settings remain on your device until you change or delete them.
- Spotify refresh tokens remain in Keychain until you disconnect Spotify or remove the app.
- You can remove connected-service data through app settings and/or uninstall the app to delete app data.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
FlipJack is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Security
We rely on Apple platform security features, including secure Keychain storage for sensitive tokens and encrypted transport where available.

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by a revised "Effective Date" above.
